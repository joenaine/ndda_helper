Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for drugs?
Use the search bar at the top of the app. You can search by drug name, ATC code, registration number, or producer name. The search is case-insensitive and works with partial matches.
How do I export selected drugs?
Tap on drug cards to select them, then use the "Export" button at the bottom right. The selected drugs will be downloaded as a CSV file with all relevant information.
How is data stored?
Drug data is cached locally on your device after the first fetch. This allows for faster loading on subsequent uses. You can manually refresh the data using the refresh button in the app bar.
Is the app free to use?
Yes, Drug Helper Kz is free to use. It provides access to publicly available drug registry data from the Kazakhstan NDDA.
